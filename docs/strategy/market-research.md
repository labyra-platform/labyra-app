### 1.2 LIMS Market — Niche Trực Tiếp Của Labrya

Global LIMS market được định giá $2.54B năm 2024, đạt $2.88B năm 2025, và dự kiến tăng trưởng CAGR 12.5%, đạt $5.19B vào 2030.

Top 4 players — LabWare, Thermo Fisher, LabVantage Solutions, STARLIMS — chiếm khoảng 80% market share. **Đây là market concentrated nhưng underserved ở academic và developing markets.**

#### Phân khúc quan trọng:

Industry-specific LIMS segment dự kiến tăng trưởng CAGR cao nhất (13%), software segment CAGR 13.1% — đây chính xác là vị trí của Labrya: **industry-specific (materials science) + cloud software**.

Cloud-based LIMS segment dự kiến đạt 57% market share vào 2035, driven by flexibility, cost-effectiveness, và remote access support.

#### Gap hiện tại trong LIMS market:

```
Thực trạng thị trường LIMS 2026:

## PHẦN 3 — ADJACENT MARKETS & EXPANSION OPPORTUNITIES

### 3.1 Horizontal Expansion — Cùng User, Khác Niche

Sau khi dominate materials science, Labrya có thể expand theo user (researcher) sang các lĩnh vực liền kề:

#### Tier 1 — Gần nhất, ít effort nhất

**Environmental Science Labs**
- Market: $1.2B global environmental testing
- Pain: Water/soil/air sample tracking, regulatory compliance
- Overlap với Labrya: Sample lineage, chemical inventory, spectrum (ICP, GC-MS)
- Adaptation effort: Medium — cần thêm environmental-specific analyzers

**Pharmaceutical / Drug Discovery Labs**
- Market: $1.8B pharma LIMS
- Pain: FDA/GMP compliance, batch tracking, stability studies
- Overlap: Experiment management, compliance layer
- Adaptation effort: High — GMP compliance phức tạp hơn

**Food & Beverage Testing Labs**
- Market: $680M globally
- Pain: Contamination testing, shelf-life studies, regulatory
- Overlap: Chemical inventory, testing protocols
- Adaptation effort: Medium

#### Tier 2 — Medium term (2–3 năm)

**Biotech / Life Sciences**
- Market: Largest LIMS segment (~40% of $5.19B)
- Pain: Sample tracking, PCR data, sequencing results
- Overlap: Experiment management, AI analysis
- Adaptation effort: High — completely different analyzer types

**University Teaching Labs**
- Market: Underserved, high volume, low budget
- Pain: Student experiment tracking, safety compliance, equipment booking
- Overlap: 80% of current Labrya features
- Adaptation effort: Low — mostly pricing/packaging change

**Contract Research Organizations (CROs)**
- Market: $100B+ globally, fastest growing LIMS segment
- Pain: Multi-client sample management, regulatory audit
- Overlap: Multi-tenant architecture (already built!)
- Adaptation effort: Medium — mostly compliance and reporting

#### Tier 3 — Long term (3–5 năm)

**Industrial / Quality Control Labs**
- Manufacturing QC, semiconductor testing
- Requires ISO 17025 certification support

**Clinical/Medical Labs**
- Requires ISO 15189, HL7/FHIR integration
- Regulated market, high compliance bar

### 3.2 Vertical Expansion — Cùng Niche, Thêm Features

**Electronic Lab Notebook (ELN)**
- $1.2B market, growing 12% CAGR
- Natural extension của experiment management
- Key players: LabArchives, Benchling, Notion (general)
- Labrya advantage: Already have experiment data model + AI

**Scientific Data Management System (SDMS)**
- $800M market
- Raw data archiving, version control, search
- Natural extension của GCS storage layer

**Laboratory Execution System (LES)**
- Real-time protocol guidance, SOP enforcement
- Step-by-step lab procedure với AI assistance

### 3.3 Geographic Expansion Path

```
Phase 1 (2026): Vietnam — first-mover, compliance advantage
Phase 2 (2027): ASEAN — Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines
  → Similar academic research culture
  → No dominant local player
  → English content sufficient
Phase 3 (2028): South Asia — India, Bangladesh
  → Massive university system
  → Price-sensitive → free tier strategy
  → Growing materials science research
Phase 4 (2029): Global academic — Europe, US
  → WCAG compliance required
  → Higher bar for features
  → Compete on price vs Benchling ($150/user)
```

### 3.4 Platform Play — Long Term Vision

Sau khi đạt critical mass, Labrya có thể evolve thành **platform**:

```
Labrya Platform (5+ năm)
├── Core: Lab Management OS
├── Marketplace: 3rd-party analyzers (plugin ecosystem)
│   → Analyzer vendors build Labrya-compatible plugins
│   → Revenue share model
├── Data: Anonymized research dataset
│   → Opt-in data contribution
│   → Sell aggregated insights to instrument vendors
│   → Train better domain models

    → Other labs apps build on top

Labrya ở **sweet spot**: high specialization + affordable price = maximum value perception.

### 7.2 Top 5 Strategic Recommendations

**1. Dominate VN academic before going regional**
Có $174K–600K ARR potential tại VN mà không cần expansion. Trước tiên win VN, dùng làm case study cho ASEAN.

**2. Compliance là moat, không chỉ là feature**
Nghị định 24/2026 compliance positioning không phải selling point — đây là entry barrier cho competitors và trust anchor cho enterprise deals. Double down.

**3. Free tier là acquisition, not charity**
Free tier phải đủ mạnh để researcher thật sự dùng hàng ngày. Nếu free tier tốt → word-of-mouth → organic growth không cần ads.

**4. Adjacent expansion: Environmental labs next**
Sau materials science, environmental testing labs tại VN là target dễ nhất (similar compliance requirements, similar user behavior, existing GCS/Firestore architecture reusable).

**5. Data moat là long-term play**
Opt-in anonymized spectrum data từ hàng nghìn labs → train better AI models → AI giỏi hơn → attract more labs → more data. Flywheel này là sustainable competitive advantage mà LabWare không có.
